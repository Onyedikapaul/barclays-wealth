The Platform provides access to a range of wealth management and financial tools, including but not limited to:

All fund transfers initiated through the Platform are subject to verification and may be reviewed for security purposes. By initiating a transfer, you confirm that:

  • You are the authorized account holder or have proper authorization

  • Sufficient funds are available or will be available at the time of processing

Once a transfer has been processed and confirmed, it may not be reversible. Please verify all details carefully before submission.

6 Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data and transactions, including:

  • 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) via mobile app
  • Activity logging and anomaly detection
  • 4-digit transaction PIN required for all financial actions
  • Session timeouts for inactive periods

While we take every reasonable precaution, no system is entirely immune to risk. We encourage all users to enable 2FA and regularly review their activity logs.

We collect personal data to provide, maintain, and improve our services. The types of data we collect include:

  • Technical data: IP address, device identifiers, browser type, session logs
  • Activity data: login history, feature usage, security events

This data is used to authenticate your identity, process transactions, comply with legal obligations, detect fraud, improve our services, and communicate with you regarding your account.
