Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How do I reset my transaction PIN? +
Go to Account Settings → Reset Transaction PIN. You'll need to verify your email address first, then set a new 4-digit PIN. The process takes less than 2 minutes.
What should I do if I notice a suspicious transaction? +
Contact our security team immediately via phone or the emergency support line. You can also freeze your account temporarily from the Account Settings page while we investigate.
How long do cross-border transfers take? +
Most cross-border transfers are processed within 1–3 business days depending on the destination country and the receiving institution's processing times.
Can I link an external card to my account? +
Yes. Navigate to "Link External Card" in the sidebar menu of your dashboard. You'll need the card number, expiry date, and CVV for verification.
How do I enable 2FA for my account? +
Go to Account Settings and toggle on "2FA Authentication". You'll be guided through the setup process using your mobile app. It takes under 3 minutes to activate.
